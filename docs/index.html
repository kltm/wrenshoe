About Wrenshoe

Wrenshoe is a passive flashcard system. Cards cycle automatically in your peripheral vision — no interaction required. It's designed for background, low-effort language study while you do other things.

Two modes

Ambient cycles cards slowly on their own. Dock your phone on a charger in landscape and glance at it throughout the day.

Flashcard lets you quiz yourself. Space = know, any other key = don't know. You get a score at the end.

Installing on iPhone

  1. In Safari, tap the Share button.
  2. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Launch Wrenshoe from the new home-screen icon — it opens full-screen.
  4. Rotate to landscape and dock on a charger for nightstand use.
